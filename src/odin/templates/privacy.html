Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-07

What we collect

  • Email address — only if you sign in. Used to send sign-in links and identify your account.
  • Anonymous cookie — a random ID stored in your browser to count daily searches before sign-in.
  • IP address — used in memory for rate limiting. Not retained after the rate-limit window expires.
  • Search queries — the text you submit. Tied to your account if signed in, otherwise to the anonymous cookie.

Where your queries go

Each query is sent to two third parties to produce a profile:

  • Anthropic (Claude API) — for categorization, query expansion, and synthesis. See Anthropic's privacy policy for their data handling.
  • SearXNG-routed search engines — your query is forwarded to Brave Search, Startpage, Qwant, and Mojeek to find sources. SearXNG is hosted by us; the engines do not see your IP.

Retention

  • Anonymous search history: 7 days.
  • Signed-in search history: 90 days.
  • Rate-limit counters: until the next UTC midnight.
  • Cached profile results: 24 hours.

Your rights

You can delete your account and all associated data from your dashboard at any time. To request access to or correction of your data, email us.

Cookies

Odin uses only essential cookies: an anonymous ID for quota tracking, a session token after sign-in, and a CSRF token. No third-party analytics or trackers.
